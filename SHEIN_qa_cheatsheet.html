Slide 1 — 框架与核心判断
1
Why do you use a full life-cycle lens instead of focusing only on production or only on the business model?
为什么用「全生命周期」视角，而不是只讲生产或只讲商业模式？
›
Slide 1 life cycle
Our question is simple: can ultra-fast fashion really be sustainable?
我们的问题很简单：超快时尚真的可持续吗？
1
My answer uses one clear logic: we must evaluate SHEIN across the whole life cycle, not only one step.
我的回答逻辑很直接：必须把 SHEIN 放在整条生命周期里评估，不能只看某一个环节。
2
So we follow four links: raw materials, production, logistics, and end-of-life.
所以我们跟四个环节：原料、生产、物流、废弃/末端。
3
If we only praise on-demand production, we may miss the high-carbon start (materials) and the pressure after use (disposal and microplastics).
如果只夸「按需生产」，就容易漏掉：上游高碳起点（材料）以及使用之后的压力（丢弃与微塑料）。

“Sustainability is about total impact along the chain — not a single ‘green’ step.”
可持续看的是链条上的总影响，不是某一个「绿色」单点。
1
What do you mean by “less inventory waste per item ≠ less total environmental impact”?
「单件库存浪费减少 ≠ 总环境影响减少」具体是什么意思？

Slide 1–2 core claim
1
Reducing unsold stock is real progress. It is a real efficiency gain.
减少滞销库存是真进步，也是真实的效率提升。
2
But sustainability is about total impact, not only per-unit efficiency.
但可持续讲的是总影响，不只是「每件更高效」。

When volume grows faster than per-unit improvement, absolute emissions can still rise. Our slide shows Scope 3 up 23.1% in 2024.
当总量涨得比「单件改进」更快时，绝对排放仍可能上升。我们幻灯片上 2024 年 Scope 3 上升了 23.1%。

“Reducing inventory waste does not mean reducing total environmental impact.”
减少库存浪费，不等于减少总环境影响。
1
What is Scope 3, and why does the +23.1% figure matter for your argument?
什么是 Scope 3？+23.1% 这个数字对你的论证为什么重要？

Slide 1–2 Scope 3
1
Scope 3 covers indirect emissions across the value chain — for example partners’ manufacturing, logistics, and other upstream/downstream sources, depending on how the firm reports.
Scope 3 指价值链上的间接排放，例如合作工厂生产、物流等上下游来源（具体边界看企业披露方式）。
2
On our slide, Scope 3 is about 26.2 million tonnes CO₂e (2024), up 23.1% year on year.
我们幻灯片上：Scope 3 约 2620 万吨二氧化碳当量（2024），同比 +23.1%。
3
We use it to show: efficiency stories do not automatically win if total scale is still rising fast.
用它说明：只要总量仍在快速扩张，「效率故事」本身推不出总影响在下降。
一句收束 · One line
“The number is from SHEIN’s own 2024 report — even with efficiency measures, the total carbon footprint grew.”
数字来自 SHEIN 自己 2024 年报告——即便有「提效」叙事，总碳足迹仍在增长。
Slide 2 — 四环节：措施与局限
2
SHEIN plans more recycled polyester by 2030. Doesn’t that solve the materials problem?
SHEIN 计划 2030 年提高再生聚酯比例，这不就解决材料问题了吗？
›
Slide 2polyester
1
The target matters: 31% recycled polyester by 2030 — that is a real measure.
目标本身有意义：2030 年 31% 再生聚酯，这是实打实的措施。
2
But today the mix is still dominated by about 82% petroleum-based polyester.
但当前结构仍是约 82% 石油基聚酯为主。
3
Polyester is fossil-fuel based and hard to degrade, so the system still starts with a high-carbon foundation.
聚酯来自化石燃料且难降解，所以系统起点仍是高碳基础。
4
Our slide also notes chemical risks: some tested products exceed EU REACH limits — materials are not only a carbon story.
幻灯片还提到化学品风险：部分抽检产品超过欧盟 REACH 限值——材料不只是碳的问题。
一句收束 · One line
“Targets are welcome — but the baseline today is still mostly virgin polyester.”
欢迎目标，但今天的底色仍主要是原生聚酯。
2
Isn’t the on-demand, small-batch model the definition of sustainable fashion?
「按需、小单」不就是可持续时尚吗？
›
Slide 2on-demand
1
Yes — it can reduce unsold stock. The first batch is small, around 100–200 units. That is a real efficiency gain.
对——它能减少滞销；首单约 100–200 件，这是真实效率提升。
2
But sustainability is about total impact. On the slide, manufacturing plus transport is about 96% of total emissions.
但可持续看总影响。我们片上制造+运输约占总排放 96%。
3
We do not deny the improvement. We say: do not confuse “less waste per item” with “less total harm” when output keeps scaling.
我们不否认进步。要说清：别把「每件浪费更少」当成「总伤害更小」，尤其在产量仍在放大时。
一句收束 · One line
“On-demand cuts unsold inventory — it does not automatically cap total production or total emissions.”
按需减少滞销，但不会自动限制总产量或总排放。
2
How do logistics fit your critique if SHEIN plans to localise production?
SHEIN 说要本地化生产，你还怎么批评物流这一块？
›
Slide 2logistics
1
The measure side is fair: SHEIN plans to move production closer to consumers.
措施面要承认：SHEIN 计划把生产挪近消费者。
2
The limitation side is still heavy: cross-border small-parcel air freight to many markets; transport emissions rose about 13.7% to roughly 8.5 million tonnes.
局限面仍然重：跨境小包裹空运到很多市场；运输排放升约 13.7%，到约 850 万吨。
3
In our wording: speed and convenience still sit in tension with low transport emissions.
用我们的话说：用户要的快与方便，和低运输排放之间仍有张力。
一句收束 · One line
“Plans are not outcomes yet — today’s model still relies on high-carbon shipping at scale.”
计划不等于结果——当下模式仍大规模依赖高碳运输。
2
Why spend time on end-of-life if SHEIN has a waste fund and a resale platform?
既然有废料基金和二手平台，为什么还要讲末端/废弃？
›
Slide 2end-of-life
1
Those measures are real: a textile waste fund and peer-to-peer resale.
这些措施是真的：纺织废料基金、C2C 转售平台。
2
But many items are low-price and short-use, so disposal still happens fast.
但很多单品低价、短用，丢弃仍然很快。
3
Synthetic textiles tie to microplastics — washing and disposal do not stop at checkout. Our slide cites evidence on microfibre release (e.g. up to 700K microfibres per wash).
化纤联系微塑料——洗涤和丢弃不会在结账处结束。我们片引用文献里单次洗涤可释放多达 70 万根微纤维等证据。
4
We say on the slide: this may address symptoms, not the upstream high-turnover model.
片上的判断：这更像治症状，没有改上游「高周转」模式。
一句收束 · One line
“Environmental pressure continues after purchase — fast disposal and microplastics are part of the same system.”
买单之后环境压力仍在——快丢弃和微塑料是同一套系统里的问题。
Slide 3 — 治理、TPB、建议
3
You write “policy exists ≠ enforcement.” What evidence do you point to on the slide?

Slide 3BEUC
1
A simple rule on this slide is: policy on paper is not the same as effective enforcement.
片上一条简单规则：纸面政策 ≠ 有效执行。
2
In the EU, authorities raised concerns about misleading sustainability claims and deceptive labels.
在欧盟，监管方关注误导性可持续宣传与欺骗性标签。
3
Groups like BEUC criticise dark patterns — fake countdowns, low-stock pressure, confirm-shaming. These nudges push people to buy more.
BEUC 等批评暗黑模式：假倒计时、低库存施压、confirm-shaming 等，都在把人往「多买」推。
一句收束 · One line
“Rules and complaints show the gap between corporate messaging and how consumers are actually nudged.”
规则与投诉说明：企业话术和用户实际被引导的方式之间有缝隙。
3
Why use the Theory of Planned Behaviour here? Isn’t it too psychology-heavy for an environmental analysis?
为什么这里用「计划行为理论」TPB？环境分析会不会太「心理学」？
›
Slide 3TPB
1
We use it as a simple map — not to replace life-cycle data, but to explain why demand stays hot.
把它当简单地图：不替代生命周期数据，用来解释需求为什么一直旺。
2
Attitude: products feel cheap and easy to justify.
态度：觉得便宜、容易自我合理化。
3
Subjective norms: trends and FOMO create pressure to keep up.
主观规范：潮流与「怕错过」带来跟风压力。
4
Perceived control: the app makes ordering fast and frictionless.
知觉行为控制：App 让下单又快又顺滑。
5
Together these drivers reinforce overconsumption, often by design. That links governance to environmental outcomes.
合在一起强化过度消费，且常常是设计出来的——这样就把治理和环境结果连起来了。
一句收束 · One line
“TPB helps explain demand-side pressure — without demand, the upstream volume story does not close.”
TPB 解释需求侧压力——没有需求端，上游「放量」的故事说不圆。
3
Your recommendations include consumers. Isn’t that unfair — blaming shoppers instead of the company?
建议里还有消费者，是不是在甩锅给买家、不公平？
›
Slide 3consumer
1
We do not say consumers carry the whole blame. Our slide uses three levels: enterprise, regulator, consumer.
我们没有说责任全在消费者。片上是三层：企业、监管者、消费者。
2
For consumers, we stress transparent information and a shift from volume to value.
对消费者侧重：信息透明、从买得多转向重价值。
3
We also say choices should be supported by fair pricing — better options must be realistic, not only a moral duty on individuals.
还要强调：公平定价支撑选择——好选项要现实可行，不能只剩「道德绑架个人」。
一句收束 · One line
“Change needs firms, rules, and everyday behaviour together — not guilt-tripping shoppers alone.”
改变要企业+规则+日常行为一起，不能只靠让买家内疚。
3
In one sentence, what is your bottom line about SHEIN and sustainability?
一句话：你对 SHEIN 与可持续的底线结论是什么？
›
Slide 3conclusion
1
Fast fashion is a full life-cycle problem — from materials to disposal.
快时尚是全生命周期问题——从材料到丢弃。
2
SHEIN optimises the wrong metric if we only celebrate efficiency of waste per item, not reduction of waste and scale at system level.
若只庆祝「每件浪费效率」，却在系统层面回避减量与规模，就是在优化错指标。
3
Real change needs coordinated moves across enterprise, regulation, and consumption — not a single fix.
真正转型要企业、监管、消费协同，不是单一招数。
一句收束 · One line
“Efficiency of waste is not the same as reduction of waste.”
「浪费的效率」不等于「浪费的减少」。
